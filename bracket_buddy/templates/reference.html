Statistics Reference


Adjusted EM (Adjusted Efficiency Margin)

    Adjusted Offensive Efficiency - Adjusted Defensive Efficiency, ideally the overall gauge of a team's quality.

Adjusted D Efficiency

    Number of points a team allows per 100 defensive possessions, normalized for strength of schedule

Adjusted O Efficiency

    Number of points a team scores per 100 offensive possessions, normalized for strength of schedule

Adjusted Tempo

    The number of possessions per 40 minutes (officially FGA-OR+TO+0.42*FTA) adjusted for an opponent's pace of play

Assist Rate

    The percentage of a team's field goals which are assisted

Bench

    Percentage of minutes played by non-starters (players playing less than 10% of games not included)

Block %

    Percentage of opponent's shots which a team blocks

D Efficiency (Defensive Efficiency)

    Number of points a team allows per 100 possessions (unadjusted for opponent)

D Free Throws

    Overall percentage of points that a team allows from free throws

D 3 Pointers

    Overall percentage of points that a team allows from 3 point FGs

D 2 Pointers

    Overall percentage of points that a team allows from 2 point FGs

D Turnover %

    Percentage of defensive possessions in which a team forces a turnover

Effective FG% D

    A teams field goal percentage defense with 50% extra weight for 3 Pt field goals allowed ((.5*3FGM + FGM) / FGA)

Effective FG% O

    A team's field goal percentage with 50% extra weight for 3 Pt FGs ((.5*3FGM + FGM) / FGA)

Experience

    The average experience of rostered players, weighted for minutes played (0= freshman, 1= sophomore, 2= junior, 3= senior)

Free Throw Rate D

    Opponent's number of free throws per field goal attempt (FTA/FGA)

Free Throw Rate O

    Number of free throws a team shoots per field goal attempt (FTA/FGA)

Free Throw %

    A team's free throw percentage (free throws made/free throw attempts)

O Efficiency

    Points scored per 100 possessions (unadjusted)

O Free Throws

    Overall percentage of points a team scores from free throws

O Rebound % Allowed

    Percentage of opponent's missed shots that the opponent rebound

O Rebound % Earned

    Overall percentage of a team's missed shots they are able to rebound

O 3 Pointers

    Overall percentage of points scored by 3 Pt FGs

O 2 Pointers

    Overall percentage of points scored by 2 Pt FGs

O Turnover %

    Percentage of possessions in which a team commits a turnover

Opponent Assist Rate

    Opponent's percentage of FGs in which the FG was assisted

Opponent Block %

    Percentage of shots blocked by the opponent

Opponent Free Throw %

    Opponent free throw percentage (free throws made/free throws attempted)

Opponent 3 Pt FG%

    Opponent's 3 Pt FG% (three pointers made/three pointers attempted)

Opponent 2 Pt FG%

    Opponent's 2 Pt FG% (two pointers made/two pointers attempted)

Opponent Steal Rate

    Percentage of possessions in which a team has the ball stolen by the opponent

Size

    Average height of players in inches (doesn't account for players playing less than 10% of minutes)

Steal Rate

    Percentage of defensive possessions in which a team gets a steal

Tempo

    Number of possessions per 40 minutes (unadjusted)

3 Pt FG%

    Percentage of made 3 Pt FGs (three pointers made/three pointers attempted)

2 Pt FG%

    Percentage of made 2 Pt FGs (two pointers made/two pointers attempted)
